# Frequently Asked Questions

The following are questions that I have gotten a lot, so I am listing them out here to answer them all in the same place.

## When will this mod be released?
Although the exact date is unknown, I plan on releasing this mod shortly after the 1.21.11 version of Palladium is released to the public. Since the intended purpose of the mod is to provide a framework for other quirk related addons, you can expect this to release in stages, with each version adding new tools and abilities to take advantage of.

## What Minecraft version is this mod for?
"Your Hero Academia" will be released for 1.21.11 on Neoforge. 

## What will happen to Bandit's Quirk Lib (1.20.1)
Eventually, the plan is to generalize "Your Hero Academia" enough that I can port everything back to the 1.20.1. However, this is not planned for anytime soon, as the focus will be on the 1.21.11 version.

## What quirks are you going to be adding?
I have no idea. Ideally, I will add quirks in as I find the time to. My main focus will be on perfecting the base systems, and ensuring that the abilities I am creating are useful to those of you creating addons. Eventually there will be a lot of quirks inside the main mod. I just can't say when.
